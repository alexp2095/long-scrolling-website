The Race of a Lifetime

The reason as to why I decided on this idea was because I love running and I have been doing it for years now. However, I actally was not always into runninng until my parents gave me running shoes in 2012. That day after, I ran and ran until I couldn't run anymore. Now, I participate in various running races, allowing me to further develop my skills. It has now become not just an activity, but a lifetime opportunity for me to change myself for the better I decided to title it The Race of a Lifetime simply because it accurately fitted how I feel about running and completing races. Since I was the only one who created the website and idea, I would be the sole author.
